About

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at Yale University, advised by Professor Michael Higley. I am interested in understanding how sensory information is transformed into behavior and cognition through neuroimaging and computational approaches. I am also a Wu Tsai Graduate Fellow.

  • FocusNeuroimaging & computational neuroscience
  • ScalesSynapse → circuit → whole brain
